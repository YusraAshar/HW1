What Does an Operating System *Even* Do?

The OS is like the behind-the-scenes manager that keeps your tech world from falling apart. Here are the ✨core functions✨:

  • Process Management: Multitasking maestro 🧠
  • Memory Management: Keeps things tidy so your apps don’t fight for space 🧹
  • File System Management: Organizes your stuff like folders, files, and more 📁
  • Device Management: Talks to your mouse, printer, and all that jazz 🎧🖨
  • Security & Access Control: Keeps intruders out and your data safe 🔒
  • User Interface: The pretty face you click and tap 🎨
